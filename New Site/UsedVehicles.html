Used Vehicle Safety Reviews

Informed decisions for safer journeys. We review used vehicles up to 12 years old for safety, technology, and value.

2015 Volkswagen Polo 1.2 TSI
R 150,000 - R 180,000

A perennial favorite, the Polo offers a solid build, good standard safety features for its time, and excellent value retention.

Bargain Buy Good Value
2014 Volvo V40 D4
R 190,000 - R 220,000

A standout for safety, featuring City Safety auto-braking as standard. A used V40 is one of the safest cars in its price bracket.

Safety Pick High Tech
