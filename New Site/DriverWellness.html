Health & Safety

Driver Wellness

Take care of yourself - your health and alertness are crucial for safe driving. Learn essential wellness practices for drivers.

Essential Driver Wellness Guidelines

Drivers should make sure that they get plenty of rest before tackling a long journey. They should also look after their health, eat well and stay fit.

Driver fatigue and lack of attention can significantly increase the risk of a crash. Drivers must stay healthy, sober and well rested,

OR THEY SHOULD NOT DRIVE!

Always Maintain Your Vehicle

All vehicles need to be roadworthy, inspect the vehicle before each trip, and check the brakes and lights regularly during the journey.

Brake defects are the most frequently cited out-of-service inspection violation. Learn how to inspect brakes, identify safety defects, and get them repaired before risking your life, and others.

Quick Wellness Tips
Get Adequate Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep before long drives

Stay Hydrated

Drink water regularly, avoid excessive caffeine

Take Regular Breaks

Stop every 2 hours for 15-20 minutes

Avoid Substances

Never drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs
