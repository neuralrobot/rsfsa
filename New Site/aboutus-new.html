Our Mission

Mission Statement

The Road Safety Foundation South Africa is committed to reducing the number of crashes and resulting injuries and fatalities on our roads. Collaborating with Government entities and developing strategic partnerships with the Private Sector, the Foundation strives to introduce an acceptable and efficient mechanism of making our roads safer.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work and commitment to road safety.

Safety First

We prioritize the safety and well-being of all road users, advocating for evidence-based solutions that protect lives.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships between public and private sectors to create meaningful change.

Education

We promote road safety awareness through comprehensive education and training programs for all communities.

Innovation

We embrace innovative approaches and technologies to develop effective road safety solutions.

Community

We are committed to serving and empowering communities across South Africa through targeted programs.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and ethical practices in all our initiatives and partnerships.

Our Journey

A timeline of our foundation's growth and impact over the years.

1999

Road Safety Foundation South Africa was established with a vision to reduce road traffic fatalities and injuries.

2005

Launched our first major partnership with government entities to implement nationwide safety campaigns.

2010

Established the KWiKS program, providing safety equipment to underprivileged school children.

2015

Expanded our reach to all nine provinces, making us a truly national foundation.

2020

Adapted our programs for digital delivery during the pandemic, maintaining our impact despite challenges.

2025

Continuing to innovate and expand our impact, working towards a future with zero road traffic fatalities.
