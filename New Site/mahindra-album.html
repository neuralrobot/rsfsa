About Our Partnership

Mahindra & Mahindra is a leading Indian automotive manufacturer with a strong presence in South Africa, committed to advancing road safety through innovative vehicle technologies and community engagement.

Vehicle Safety Innovation

Mahindra's commitment to incorporating advanced safety features in utility vehicles, passenger cars, and commercial vehicles that protect South African families.

Community Engagement

Supporting rural and community road safety initiatives, driver education programs, and awareness campaigns across South Africa.

Sustainable Mobility

Promoting environmentally responsible transportation solutions while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Excellence in Manufacturing

Delivering world-class vehicles that meet international safety standards and contribute to reducing road accidents.
