Community Medical Services

Providing critical medical services and emergency response to support road safety and community health across South Africa.

Community Medical Services & Road Safety

Community Medical Services (CMS) plays a vital role in road safety by providing immediate emergency medical response, trauma care, and health services that save lives on South African roads.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive emergency medical services, trauma care, and community health support that directly contributes to reducing road traffic fatalities and improving outcomes for accident victims across South Africa.

Our partnership with the Road Safety Foundation ensures that medical response capabilities are aligned with road safety initiatives, creating a comprehensive approach to saving lives on our roads.

Emergency Medical Services

Critical care when every second counts

Ambulance Services

24/7 emergency ambulance response with advanced life support capabilities for road traffic accidents and medical emergencies.

Trauma Care

Specialized trauma response teams trained in managing severe injuries from vehicle accidents and providing critical care en route to hospitals.

Rapid Response

Quick response times to accident scenes with fully equipped medical teams and coordination with traffic authorities and hospitals.

Medical Documentation

Comprehensive medical documentation and reporting for insurance claims, legal proceedings, and road safety analysis.

Community Outreach

Health education programs, first aid training, and community wellness initiatives focused on road safety and injury prevention.

Advanced Equipment

State-of-the-art medical equipment including defibrillators, ventilators, and monitoring systems for optimal patient care.

Emergency Contact Information

Emergency Numbers:
  • Emergency Services: 10177
  • Ambulance: 10177
  • Police: 10111
  • Fire Department: 10177
  • Traffic Police: 10111
  • Crisis Helpline: 0861 322 322

In case of a road traffic accident, call emergency services immediately. Provide clear location details and describe the nature of injuries.

Community Health & Safety Programs

Our Programs Include:
  • First Aid Training - Basic life support and emergency response
  • CPR Certification - Cardiopulmonary resuscitation training
  • Road Safety Education - Injury prevention and awareness
  • Community Screenings - Health assessments and checkups
  • Mental Health Support - Trauma counseling and support
  • Health Workshops - Wellness and prevention programs

Our community health programs are designed to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to respond effectively in emergency situations and promote overall road safety awareness.

Emergency Medical Support
