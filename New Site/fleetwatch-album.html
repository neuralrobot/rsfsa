About Our Partnership

FleetWatch is South Africa's leading transport industry publication, committed to advancing commercial vehicle safety through comprehensive reporting, industry insights, and educational initiatives across the transport sector.

Commercial Vehicle Focus

FleetWatch's dedication to promoting safety standards for trucks, buses, and commercial fleets that form the backbone of South Africa's transport infrastructure.

Industry Communication

Providing critical news, analysis, and safety information to transport operators, fleet managers, and industry stakeholders across Southern Africa.

Driver Education

Supporting professional driver training programs and safety awareness campaigns to improve commercial vehicle operation standards.

Safety Advocacy

Championing road safety initiatives and regulatory compliance in the commercial transport sector to protect all road users.
