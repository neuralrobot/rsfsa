About Our Partnership

Old Mutual Insure is a leading South African insurance provider committed to protecting lives and property while actively promoting road safety through comprehensive insurance solutions and community education programs.

Vehicle Insurance Excellence

Comprehensive vehicle insurance solutions that protect South African motorists while promoting responsible driving and road safety awareness through innovative coverage options and risk management programs.

Safety Education Programs

Educational initiatives focused on driver safety training, accident prevention, and community outreach programs that help reduce road incidents and promote safer driving practices across South Africa.

Community Engagement

Active participation in road safety campaigns and community programs that support the Road Safety Foundation's mission to reduce road fatalities and create safer transportation environments.

Data-Driven Safety

Leveraging insurance data and analytics to identify risk patterns, support safety research, and develop evidence-based strategies for reducing road incidents and improving overall traffic safety.

Partnership Impact

Through our partnership with the Road Safety Foundation, Old Mutual Insure combines insurance expertise with road safety advocacy, creating comprehensive solutions that protect both individual motorists and the broader South African community.
