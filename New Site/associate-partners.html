Associate Partners

Strategic collaborations driving road safety awareness and education

Associate Partnership Program

Our Associate Partners are organizations that share our commitment to road safety and work with us to create awareness, educate communities, and implement safety initiatives. These partnerships allow us to extend our reach and amplify our impact across different sectors and communities.

Become a Partner

Ready to join our mission? Contact us to learn more about partnership opportunities.

Our Associate Partners

Fleet Watch

A community-driven initiative promoting road safety awareness and responsible driving behavior through social media engagement and public reporting.

Visit Page
MasterDrive

Leading provider of advanced driver training programs including defensive driving, truck roll-over prevention, and motorcycle safety training across South Africa.

Visit Website
FleetMax Africa

Fleet management and logistics solutions provider focused on improving commercial vehicle safety and operational efficiency across Africa.

Visit Page
N3 Toll Concession

Operates and maintains the N3 Toll Route, connecting major provinces and promoting road safety along one of South Africa's busiest transport corridors.

Visit Website
SANRAL

South African National Roads Agency responsible for the planning, design, construction, maintenance and management of the national road network.

Visit Website
RTMC

Road Traffic Management Corporation - the lead road safety agency in South Africa, coordinating strategic planning and law enforcement across all levels of government.

Visit Website
Mercedes-Benz SA

Old Mutual Insure

Partnership Benefits

  • Access to road safety educational resources and materials
  • Joint awareness campaign opportunities
  • Training and capacity building programs
  • Networking with other safety-focused organizations
  • Recognition and visibility for safety initiatives

Become an Associate Partner

Are you interested in joining our Associate Partner network? We welcome organizations that share our vision of safer roads for everyone.

Partnership Criteria:
  • Commitment to road safety values
  • Willingness to collaborate on initiatives
  • Ability to reach and engage communities
  • Alignment with our mission and values
