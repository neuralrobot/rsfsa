Understanding Your Vehicle's Safety Features

Active Safety

These systems work to prevent accidents before they happen. They monitor your driving and the environment around your vehicle, providing warnings or taking action when necessary.

Passive Safety

These systems protect occupants when an accident occurs. They include structural design elements and restraint systems that minimize injury during a collision.

Driver Assistance

These technologies help reduce driver fatigue and improve convenience while maintaining safety. They can assist with parking, lane keeping, and cruise control.

South African Context

All safety ratings and features are relevant to South African road conditions and regulatory requirements. Ensure your vehicle meets local safety standards.
